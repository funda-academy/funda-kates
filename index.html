FAQ

Common questions −

Everything you need to know before registering.

When does Cohort 1 run?
4–28 August 2026. Live sessions every Monday and Thursday, 18:00–19:30 SAST.
What if I miss a session?
All sessions are recorded and made available after each class.
What do I need to know before joining?
Basic Linux command-line comfort and some software development experience. You don't need prior Kubernetes knowledge.
Do I get a certificate?
Yes. A certificate of completion is issued to every participant who completes the capstone project.
How do I secure my spot?
Register your interest above. You'll receive an invoice by return — your place is confirmed once payment is received. Places are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.
Why is the student price lower?
We want the programme to be accessible. Students pay R1,100 with a valid student card. Professionals pay R1,500. Same course, same certificate.
